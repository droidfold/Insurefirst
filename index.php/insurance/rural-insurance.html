Rural Insurance

Rural India is often faced with risks linked to lifestyle of people living there. There is a growing need for the Rural mass to be educated on insurance solutions that will help them take care of the risks be it their automobiles, health, property or livestock.

InsureFirst Goa offers a variety of Rural Insurance products covering risks of this nature.

Rural Insurance Plans


Agriculture Insurance

For Indian agriculture to make up for lost time, changes will be needed, and rural insurance are key elements that can help in this regard.

Cattle Insurance

An insurance policy to provide insurance to cattle-breeding community of rural India to cover their Cows / Buffaloes; thus providing source a financial protection in case of death or disability caused to cattle due to accident or disease.

Rural Insurance Features and Benefits

Plantation and horticultural crops including grapes, rubber trees etc. Sub-animals including honeybee, silkworm etc. Property. For example agricultural pumpsets, etc.

Why is Rural Insurance Scheme required?

The main objective of the programme is to make sure that the rural families involved are provided with working capital and assistance.
